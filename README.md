# 🧶 Weave

**Weave** is a local-first knowledge synthesis engine designed to bridge the gap between structured note-taking and generative audio intelligence. It takes the interconnected "graph" philosophy of Obsidian and supercharges it with the RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) and podcast-generation capabilities of Gemini.

### 🧠 The Core Concept
Your notes are **threads**. **Weave** is the loom that pulls them together. By utilizing the Gemini API, the application allows you to converse with your local vault, surface hidden connections, and transform complex topics into digestible, high-fidelity audio discussions.

### ✨ Features
* **Knowledge Graph:** A visual, node-based architecture for managing Markdown notes.
* **Gemini-Powered RAG:** Deep, source-grounded retrieval. Ask questions across your entire vault with the context and accuracy of Gemini.
* **Audio Briefs (The Podcast):** Transform any selection of nodes or search results into a dual-host podcast style discussion, perfect for reviewing your research on the go.
* **Bring Your Own Key:** Complete control over your data and costs. Simply plug in your Gemini API key and start weaving.
* **Local-First:** Designed for privacy and speed, keeping your intellectual property where it belongs.
